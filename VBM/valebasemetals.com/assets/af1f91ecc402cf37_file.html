About the Project

Project 118 is a high-grade copper resource that remains open at depth. The project is currently in the feasibility stage.

Location

Vale Base Metals’ Sossego Complex in an area known as the South Hub, approximately 10 km west of Sossego within the Carajás National Forest.

Key Metrics

  • Projected Production
    Copper: 60 kt/year*

*Estimated annual average

  • Mine Plan 

    The project involves open pit mining, a sulfide waste stockpile, an oxidized waste stockpile, a sediment dike, a ROM (Run-of-Mine) transportation system by off-road trucks to Sossego, and administrative and industrial facilities.

  • Strategy 

    Provide high-grade feed to Sossego with the aim of maintaining and/or increasing plant capacity.

  • Next Steps 

    Both 118 and Cristalino are
    advancing through feasibility studies.
