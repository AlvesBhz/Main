Co-ops and Internships 

Our co-op and internship positions are exciting opportunities for students to learn about and support our business across our Canadian operations.

Vale Base Metals provides a creative and dynamic work environment that contributes to a continuous learning experience for co-op students and interns. These opportunities allow students to grow both professionally and personally in a cutting-edge business. We look for talent who are excited to learn, bring new ideas and creative solutions to our business.

Vale Base Metals welcomes co-op and other students in many exciting disciplines such as:​

  • Engineering (Chemical, Electrical, Mining, Mineral Processing and Civil, amongst others)
  • Geology
  • Trades (Heavy Duty Technicians, Millwrights, Electricians and Mechanics, amongst others)
  • Corporate functions such as Business, Human Resources, Procurement, Supply Chain and Finance

Applicants are encouraged to review our career portal as positions are posted on an ongoing basis.

Engineer in Training 

At Vale Base Metals, engineering graduates will receive guidance and assistance to accelerate their personal development.

The EIT position provides engineering students and recent graduates with acceptable engineering work experience as they pursue their P.Eng licensing.

They will learn about our business and culture, while being exposed to different engineering methods and technical skills they require for their specialized engineering field. EITs will be encouraged to share innovative ideas to transform the business.

What are we looking for?

  • Applicants must have completed or be currently enrolled in an Engineering Undergraduate program.
  • Registered or eligible to register with a professional engineering association within Canada as part of the licensed pathway toward Professional Engineer (P.Eng.) designation.
  • Fast learner with a desire to grow professionally and personally.
  • Experience with engineering design software specific to the engineering field of study.
  • Excellent organizational and time management skills.

Applicants are encouraged to review our career portal as positions are posted on an ongoing basis.

Geologist in Training 

Vale Base Metals Geologist-in-Training (GIT) position provides world-class training and development in a well-established training program across Vale operations in Canada.

This is a developmental technical position that will allow the right candidate to shape our mining operations as well as grow into a technical leadership role.

What are we looking for?

  • Applicants must have completed a Geology or Geological Engineering degree.
  • Registered or eligible to register with a professional geoscience association in Canada as part of the pathway toward Professional Geoscientist (P.Geo.) designation.
  • Fast learner with a desire to grow professionally and personally.
  • Committed to working safely in a team or independent setting.
  • Strong problem solving and data analysis skills.

Applicants are encouraged to review our career portal as positions are posted on an ongoing basis.

Experienced Professionals

At Vale Base Metals, there’s no shortage of exciting career profiles to explore.

Production

Production workers include our Miners and Operators. Miners are responsible for drilling, blasting, operating a variety of mining machinery and constructing and maintaining drifts, passageways and shafts in our mining operations. Operators monitor, process, control machinery and equipment to manage the processing of mineral ores, molten metals, electrolytic solutions and nickel metal.

Maintenance 

Maintenance work includes millwrights, heavy duty equipment technicians, industrial electricians, machinists, welders and masons. These positions are vital to keeping mining operations running smoothly and safely, often requiring technical certifications and hands-on experience in industrial environments.

Supervisors and Superintendents

These are our frontline leaders. As a supervisor, you may be accountable for planning, scheduling and/or supervising a crew while as a Superintendent, you will lead a team of supervisors.

They have a key leadership role in mentoring teams and ensuring that projects progress on time and on budget. Although previous experience in mining or an industrial setting is considered an asset, we also appreciate that diversity of thought comes from our differences – and that’s what makes us great.

Engineering

Vale Base Metals has many specialist careers in the fields of science and engineering.

Our mining engineers design the development of mines and supervise the extraction of ore whereas civil engineers design and manage projects for the construction or repair of buildings, roads, facilities and services. We also hire chemical, metallurgical, mechanical, electrical, and other engineering professionals to help drive the efficient and profitable operation of our mining sites.   Geologists and geophysicists carry out exploration and data analysis to learn more about the structure and composition of our ore bodies. They work co-operatively with our mining engineers to plan and do mineral extraction. Chemists/analysts perform analysis of geological materials such as drill cores and ore samples, as well as liquid and solid process samples, to ensure that our products meet or exceed the excellent standards our global customers expect from Vale.

Corporate Functions

From accountants and business professionals who oversee financial forecasting, capital allocation, acquisitions, and investments, to information technology professionals who plan and direct national or global IT projects, there is no shortage of opportunities for you here at Vale Base Metals.

Recruitment and Selection FAQ

1 – How do I apply for a position? 

To apply for a position, visit our Job Portal to view and select from the positions listed. You can make use of the search feature to narrow down the options, or you can scroll through the list to see what is available.

2- I applied for a job, who can I contact? 

We appreciate the time you have taken to apply. Your application will be reviewed, and you will receive further contact or feedback from a recruiter. We ask that you also monitor your junk/spam as emails sometimes end up there.

3 – Do I have to apply to each opportunity as they come up to be considered by Vale Base Metals?

We encourage you to proactively apply to those positions that interest you the most. However, if you have applied to any position previously, your application remains in our database and is searchable by our recruiters.

4 – I don’t see a position I’m interested in applying to right now, but I’d like to be informed when new postings become available. What should I do? 

We recommend you visit our Job Portal. At the top right corner of the page, you will see an option to ‘Join Talent Network’. Click the link and fill out your details to join the community. Once you do so, your information will be available to Vale recruiters who are able to contact you should you be a good fit for future opportunities.

5 – What does Vale Base Metals look for in candidates? How can I differentiate myself from other applicants?

We look for candidates whose values align with Vale’s and encourage you to share how you will contribute to living by our values in the role that you are applying for. Also ensure you highlight  your skills, capabilities and experience based on the requirements of the job as listed in the posting.

6 – Can I edit or update my application once I apply?

Once you submit your application, you are not able to edit or update your application.

7 – How do I upload additional documents when applying (e.g., my transcript or cover letter)?

You are not required to provide any additional documentation when applying. If you are invited for an interview with any member of the hiring team, and additional documentation is required, a recruiter will request such from you.

8 – Does Vale offer relocation assistance?

Vale Base Metals offers a comprehensive relocation package to qualified new hires.

9 – Does Vale provide summer placement to students in Canada?

Please view our Early Career programs above and visit our Job Portal to view our current job opportunities for students and new graduates.
