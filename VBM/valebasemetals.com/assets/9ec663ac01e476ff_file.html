Vale Base MetalsOur Operations

Our mineral resources, infrastructure and geographic diversity set us apart.

We steward industry-leading copper and nickel reserves in Canada, Brazil and Indonesia.

Our mix of geology and geography positions us to serve customers worldwide — in Europe, the Americas and Asia — with a secure, responsible supply of high-quality, next-generation products.

Our portfolio and resources are key strengths, but to stay competitive we must be agile and deliver with confidence.

Our ongoing shift to a decentralized model empowers leaders to run operations with autonomy, ensuring resilience and capital discipline to weather any cycle.
